  • Emotional Bias: Words designed to trigger strong reactions
  • Gender Bias: Stereotypes based on gender
  • Exaggeration Bias: Absolute terms that overstate situations
  • Negativity Bias: Disproportionate focus on negative aspects
  • Confirmation Bias: Assumes agreement or dismisses alternatives
  • Religious/Ethnic Bias: Stereotypes about religious or ethnic groups
  • Age Bias: Age-related stereotypes or discriminatory language
  • Racial Bias: References to race that may perpetuate stereotypes
